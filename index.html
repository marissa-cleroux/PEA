Introduction { Objective 1 }

As a student of the Computer Science program at Heritage College I, Marissa Cleroux, am required to complete a Program Exit Assessment (PEA). The purpose of the PEA is to show that I have completed the objectives outlined by Heritage College to graduate from the Computer Science program. This website fulfills the objectives by giving a platform for my PEA. The navigation menu can be found at the top of the screen, where you can access each part of my PEA. Please visit my resume to get a better idea of my overall experience and skills acquired in the Computer Science program.

PEA Project { Objective 1 }

In the last year of the Computer Science program all students take part in designing and developing a real-world system. I was assigned, along with my group, to create a system for the Nursing Department at Heritage College. My teammates were Aaditya Chopra, Maxwell Desrosiers, Manuel Nieves-Ventoso, and Tristan Young. Our Project Manager was Richard Chan. The system enables students to keep track of the skills they have learned and practiced throughout the Nursing program. Teachers use the system to ensure students have completed the required skills, and to give the students feedback.

Applied Experience { Objective 14 }

As part of the Computer Science program there are two opportunities to complete a summer co-op. Co-ops enable students to get real-world experience in the Information Technology (IT) industry, apply what they have learned in the program, and establish contacts.

I completed my first co-op in the summer of 2018 at Shared Services Canada. I was hired as a Student Support Analyst wherein my main responsibility was to ensure the smooth deployment of applications across three networks. To read more about my first co-op, please read my co-op report.

My second co-op took place in the summer of 2019. Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC) hired me as a Student Programmer. In this co-op I was apart of the team which maintains the Grants and Contributions Online System (GCOS). My co-op report details my full experience at ESDC.
